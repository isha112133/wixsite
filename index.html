Everything we do we use our unique model

"Triple Loop Learning"

to create

"Depth & Impact"

01 SINGLE LOOP
  • Learning new skills and capabilities
  • Incremental improvement
  • Doing something better without examining or challenging underlying beliefs and assumptions.
02 DOUBLE LOOP
  • Fundamentally reshaping the underlying patterns of our thinking and behavior.
  • As a result, we’re capable of doing differently.
  • Going beyond single-loop or incremental learning.
03 TRIPLE LOOP
  • It involves “learning how to learn” by reflecting on how we learn in the first place.
  • This is about learning how our “being” influences our beliefs and thus our actions.

When we design Org wide change/transformation Interventions, we address both visible(overt) and Hidden(covert) parts of the Organization Thus creating Depth and Impact in your long-term Interventions​

Santhosh is the author of India’s first book on coaching published by Wiley and started the first coach training program in 2006 in India. Santhosh used to write a column in Business Today called “Buddha in the Boardroom.“ Santhosh coaches some CEOs and works with several leadership teams driving transformation, culture change, and leadership development. Before starting ODA, Santhosh worked as a primary school teacher in Bhutan and as an Environmental Education Officer with World Wildlife Fund.
